About DetectMapper

DetectMapper helps metal detectorists log and organise finds with photos, notes, depth, weather and map location.

Common Help

  • Make sure Camera and Location permissions are enabled in iPhone Settings if you want to save finds with photos and coordinates.
  • The free version allows up to 10 finds.
  • The Pro unlock removes the free-find limit and includes future updates.
