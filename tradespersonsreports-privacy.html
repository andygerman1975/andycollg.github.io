Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tradespersonsreports ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use the Tradespersonsreports mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Process

The App may process the following categories of information you create or provide:

  • Business profile information, such as business name, address, VAT number, phone, email, website, logo, and selected trade.
  • Customer and job information, such as customer names, addresses, report details, quotation details, photos, notes, and generated documents.
  • Subscription status information provided by Apple for in-app purchases.
  • AI input and output data you choose to submit when using AI features (for example note polishing and transcription).

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide app features, including report and quotation creation.
  • Enable backup and restore features you choose to use.
  • Verify subscription access and entitlements.
  • Provide AI-powered features only when requested by you.
  • Protect service integrity, including fair-use and anti-abuse controls.

3. Storage and Backups

Your app data is primarily stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud backup or export backup files, your data may also be stored in your iCloud account or files you choose to export.

4. AI Features and Third Parties

When you use AI features, selected text/audio content may be sent to third-party AI service providers to generate outputs. Do not submit sensitive personal information unless you are authorized to do so.

We recommend that you avoid adding special category data (for example health, biometric, or highly confidential personal information) to AI requests.

5. In-App Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect or store your full payment card details.

6. Data Retention

You control most data retention by editing or deleting records in the App and by deleting backups you created. Subscription transaction records are retained by Apple according to Apple's policies.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Your Choices

You may:

  • Edit or remove data inside the App.
  • Disable AI features by not invoking AI actions.
  • Disable backup options or remove exported backups.
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions through Apple settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date when changes are made.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@tradespersonsreports.co.uk
