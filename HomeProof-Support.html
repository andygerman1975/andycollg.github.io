Before You Contact Us

Including these details helps us solve your issue faster:

  • Device model and iOS version
  • HomeProof app version
  • What you expected vs what happened
  • Screenshots (if possible)

Common Issues

  • Calendar reminders not appearing: enable Calendar access in iOS Settings for HomeProof.
  • iCloud restore: keep the old device online to allow CloudKit sync to complete.
  • Subscription not recognized: tap Restore Purchases in app settings.

Response Times

We typically respond within 1-2 business days.

Please avoid sending sensitive personal or payment information by email.

Troubleshooting Steps

  • Close and reopen the app.
  • Restart your device.
  • Check internet connection and iCloud status.
  • Update HomeProof to the latest version.
