Fair Use Policy

Last updated: 22 April 2026

This Fair Use Policy explains how AI usage is managed in Tradespersonsreports to keep the service reliable, secure, and sustainable for all users.

1. Scope

This policy applies to AI-powered features in the App, including AI note polishing, transcription-related workflows, and AI-generated summary/action outputs.

2. Plan-Based Usage

  • Pro (£7.99/month): includes 80 AI uses per billing month.
  • Pro Unlimited (£9.99/month): marketed as unlimited, subject to fair-use and anti-abuse controls.

3. Fair-Use Controls for Unlimited

To protect service quality and prevent abuse, we may apply automated controls, including temporary rate limits where usage appears excessive or non-human.

Examples include, but are not limited to:

  • High-frequency burst activity that exceeds normal interactive usage.
  • Sustained request volumes that risk service stability or disproportionate cost.
  • Automated, scripted, or bot-driven behavior.
  • Attempts to bypass plan limits, entitlement checks, or technical controls.

4. What Happens If Fair-Use Limits Trigger

Depending on severity and recurrence, we may:

  • Temporarily delay or block AI requests.
  • Display a message requiring the user to retry later.
  • Apply stricter limits to protect the platform.

5. Prohibited Use

You must not:

  • Use the AI features in a way that violates applicable law.
  • Use automated tools to generate artificial load.
  • Attempt to reverse engineer, exploit, or disrupt service operations.

6. Policy Changes

We may update this policy as the service evolves. Material updates will be reflected by revising the "Last updated" date.

7. Contact

Questions about fair use: support@tradespersonsreports.app
